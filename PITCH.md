**PayIT — Elevator Pitch & Marketing Assets**

One-liner

- PayIT: a non-custodial Telegram-native wallet that lets users and SMEs hold USD/EUR stablecoins while transacting in local currency via seamless on/off ramps.

Target audience

- SMEs and freelancers in emerging markets who need stable-value settlement but local currency operations.
- Payment partners and FX aggregators interested in low-friction rails.
- Incubators, grants, and early-stage investors focusing on fintech for emerging markets.

Key benefits (sales points)

- Non-custodial: users control keys — reduces regulatory custody complexity and trust friction.
- Telegram-native: zero friction onboarding; leverages existing user behavior and reach.
- Instant settlement in stablecoins: avoids local FX volatility and banking delays.
- SME-first features: invoicing, payroll, recurring payments, and reporting.
- Modular onboarding: demo via testnet with points system and full feature parity for mainnet.

Suggested short description for store/landing page

- "PayIT — Hold dollars, spend locally. Non-custodial stablecoin wallets + local invoicing, all inside Telegram."

Suggested tweet / social post

- "Meet PayIT — the Telegram-native wallet that keeps your savings in USD while letting you pay and invoice in local currency. Non-custodial, instant settlement, and built for SMEs in emerging markets. Demo on testnet today. #Web3 #Fintech #Stablecoins"

Demo script (2‑3 minutes)

1. Show the onboarding flow: `/start` → select Personal → create wallet → set PIN.
2. Demonstrate sending money: type natural language "Send 3,000 NGN to Maria" → confirm → show receipt.
3. Show invoice generation and sharing: create invoice → show QR/PNG receipt preview.
4. Show testnet points (explain promotional nature) and balance query.
5. Show admin/business features: export points, broadcast message, and toggle business context.

Landing page hero copy

- Headline: "Save in stablecoins, spend in local currency — without leaving Telegram"
- Subhead: "PayIT gives businesses and individuals a non-custodial wallet to receive, hold, and manage USD/EUR stablecoins while transacting in local currency via trusted on/off ramps."
- CTA: "Demo on Testnet" (links to Telegram bot)

Assets to prepare (next actions)

- Short demo video (screen capture) — 2 minutes
- One-page PDF: architecture + customer stories
- Screenshots: onboarding, invoice, receipt PNG, admin menu
- Press kit: mission, team bios, contact email

Pricing / Go-to-market ideas

- Freemium: Basic bot features free; fees for settlement (on/off ramp partner fees) and premium business features (reporting, payroll automation)
- Partnership: revenue share with local FX/payment partners for each on/off-ramp conversion
- Enterprise: white-label solution for payment providers and banks

KPIs to track

- Activation rate (users completing `/start` and setting a PIN)
- Invoice volume (count and USD equivalent)
- On/off-ramp volume (USDC settled)
- Retention: weekly active users
- Conversion from demo to funded deployment (paid customers)
